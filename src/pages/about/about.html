About AskInsurpedia

Askinsurpedia.com is an online platform that allows people to receive answers to their insurance or wealth risk management questions 24 hours a day from verified Insurance, pension and wealth management consultants via the mobile app, SMS and web.

With the dwindling awareness of how insurance and wealth risk management operates, Askinsurpedia.com is a resource and avenue to instill confidence and educate customers and practitioners alike.

Askinsurpedia allows users send their questions via mobile app, SMS or web, then routes it to one of the verified consultants on the system who then provides an answer. This answer is then relayed to the user, and forms part of the knowledge database repository that other users can refer to a later time.
